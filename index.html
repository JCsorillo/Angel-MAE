About Me

Hey everyone! My name is Angel Mae Sabordo, and I'm a BSIT 4-B student at the College of Information and Computer Studies at Northern Iloilo State University. Born on May 2, 2003, I'm currently 21 years old, and I'm always up for a challenge.

I'm passionate about technology and love exploring the latest advancements. I find it incredibly rewarding to use my IT skills to solve everyday problems and make things more efficient. While I'm still learning, I'm always eager to expand my knowledge and improve my abilities.

I am passionate about creating unique and artistic handicrafts, turning my hobby into a rewarding journey of self-expression. My specialty lies in crafting exquisite handicraft bouquets that combine creativity and attention to detail. These beautiful, handmade arrangements make perfect gifts for any occasion, blending charm and elegance in every piece.

When I'm not busy with my studies, you can find me indulging in my favorite hobbies. I love browsing the internet, discovering new things, and getting lost in the world of anime and K-pop. There's something so captivating about both genres, from the intricate storylines and vibrant characters in anime to the catchy tunes and energetic performances in K-pop. I also enjoy taking care of my surroundings and find peace in completing household chores. It's a way for me to unwind and feel a sense of accomplishment.

I'm a friendly and approachable person, so feel free to say hi! I'm always open to meeting new people and learning from their experiences. Let's connect and explore the world of technology together!
